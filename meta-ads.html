Why most Meta accounts plateau

Meta accounts stall for predictable reasons: one creative concept recycled for months, audiences fragmented into a dozen overlapping ad sets, and pixel data polluted by junk events.

Since iOS privacy changes, Meta rewards advertisers who feed it strong creative and reliable conversion signals. Everything else — audience hacks, interest stacking — matters far less than it used to.
