The problem this solves

Why brands fail on TikTok

Most brands port their Instagram ads to TikTok, watch them die, and conclude the platform doesn't work. TikTok is a creative-first auction: the content must feel like it belongs, or the algorithm simply won't distribute it.

The second failure is measurement. TikTok drives discovery that converts later through search and direct visits — judge it on last-click alone and you'll switch off a channel that was quietly filling the funnel.
