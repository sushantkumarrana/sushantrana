When Wix is right — and when it isn't

The old objections to Wix — rigid layouts, weak SEO — largely died with Wix Studio. What remains true is that platform choice should follow business need, not developer preference.

For consultants, clinics, agencies, and local service brands, Studio delivers a professional, editable, fast-enough site at a fraction of custom-build cost. For complex commerce or heavy integrations, I'll steer you to a different stack — before you spend, not after.
