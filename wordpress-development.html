The problem this solves

The bloated-WordPress problem

The pattern is familiar: a heavyweight theme, thirty plugins doing overlapping jobs, a page builder stacking div upon div, and a site that takes six seconds to paint on a phone. Then someone suggests more plugins to fix the speed.

A lean WordPress build inverts that: a purpose-built theme, a handful of audited plugins, and content structured around what visitors came to do — which, on a business site, is to decide whether to contact you.
